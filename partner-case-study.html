Partner Marketing Manager at Prerender.io
A grounded strategy for partner identification, activation, and communication.
📍 Role: Partner Marketing Manager 🏢 Prerender.io / saas.group 📅 March 2026 🎯 Primary KPI: Partner-sourced revenue ✍️ Made by: Nané Martirosyan
Starting point I'm working from: Prerender.io has 14 solution partners and a handful of technology partners including Netlify, Oncrawl, OtterlyAI, and Virto Commerce, all signed in the past 10 months. The goal over the next 12–18 months is to build on that foundation: introducing the structure, incentives, and activation processes that turn a strong and growing partner list into a consistent, scalable acquisition channel to drive partner-sourced revenue.

Partner Identification, Qualification & Outreach
3 Solution Partners · 1 Technology Partner
The selection logic was straightforward: strong solution partners are those who regularly encounter the problem Prerender solves: not occasionally, but as a consistent part of their client work. They serve the kinds of companies Prerender is built for (mid-market to enterprise, large sites, JavaScript-heavy), and they have a clear reason to recommend a solution rather than trying to build one themselves. Generalist agencies rarely meet that bar. Specialists do.

Onely
onely.com
Solution Partner Technical SEO
Why they fit
Onely is a respected technical SEO agency globally, known specifically for their expertise in JavaScript and search visibility, which is exactly the problem Prerender solves. Their clients are enterprise brands with large, complex websites (eBay, IKEA, G2), and when Onely identifies a rendering issue during an audit, they need a solution to recommend. Prerender is the natural fit. The partnership formalizes a referral relationship that already makes sense organically.
Engagement approach
Since the company is small, reach out directly to Onely's founder on LinkedIn, they are publicly active and vocal about JavaScript SEO. Reference a relevant use case that has a touchpoint with Prerender's offering specifically in the opening message. The key selling point for Onely is that a referral agreement lets them close the loop on audits: instead of handing clients a problem without a solution, they can recommend Prerender as their vetted implementation partner. That strengthens their deliverable, not just their revenue.
StudioHawk
studiohawk.com
Solution Partner SEO Agency
Why they fit
StudioHawk is an SEO-only agency founded in Melbourne in 2015 by Harry Sanders, with 80-100+ specialists across offices in Australia, the UK, and Atlanta. They do not offer paid media, social, or web development: SEO is the entire business. Named clients include Officeworks, QuickBooks APAC, and The Good Guys, most of them non-technical buyers who act directly on StudioHawk's recommendations. On the Officeworks engagement, StudioHawk documented pre-rendering React to make JavaScript SEO-friendly, and they have a published blog post explicitly recommending Prerender.io as a reliable solution for dynamic rendering. They run a dedicated headless SEO service and have recently launched an AI SEO offering. Importantly, they operate an active partner program that explicitly invites SaaS and tech companies, with Semrush already in as a named partner.
Engagement approach
Contact StudioHawk through their partner program directly and reach out to their team on LinkedIn. Lead with the fact that StudioHawk already recommends Prerender.io in their published content: open by referencing that and asking whether formalizing it makes sense. Position Prerender as a concrete fix they can hand clients on React, Vue, or Angular sites where Googlebot is struggling to index content. Low implementation effort on their end, clear value-add to an audit they are already delivering, and a named SaaS partner arrangement that fits the program they are actively building.
FocusReactive
focusreactive.com
Solution Partner Web Dev Agency
Why they fit
Where Onely and StudioHawk diagnose rendering problems, FocusReactive builds the sites that have them. They work almost exclusively with Next.js and React, and hold certified partnerships with seven headless CMSes: Sanity, Storyblok, Contentful, Payload, DatoCMS, Directus, and Hygraph, as well as a Vercel Solution Partner designation. Named clients include EasyPark (35 domains, 18 languages), Tipico, Betway, and Secret Escapes. They sell a productized Next.js SEO Audit service and recently announced a partnership with Wordify specifically to address AI search visibility for enterprise brands. Every site they deliver is a potential Prerender customer.
Engagement approach
The entry point here is practical rather than strategic: offer their team an extended free trial on an active client project and frame it as a proof of concept. If it works for the client, it becomes part of their standard recommendation going forward.
Storyblok ✦ Recommended Technology Partner
storyblok.com
Technology Partner Headless CMS
Why Storyblok
Storyblok is one of the fastest-growing venture-backed headless CMS platforms, used by brands like Virgin Media O2, Oatly, and TomTom to build JavaScript-heavy sites, exactly the kind of sites that need Prerender. Projects built on Storyblok are potential Prerender customers. Storyblok runs an established Partner Program designed primarily for agencies and solution partners, but they have also built a genuine technology ecosystem, integrating with tools like Vercel and Algolia, and they have a documented history of co-building real projects with technology partners and publishing those as case studies. They understand the value of ecosystem partnerships and have the infrastructure to list and promote integrations. They also share Prerender's concern around AI search visibility: OtterlyAI, a company already in Prerender's own partner ecosystem, is part of the same space Storyblok is actively watching. The shared context makes the pitch straightforward: Storyblok handles the content and delivery layer; Prerender ensures that content is fully visible to search engines and AI crawlers.
Engagement approach
Reach out directly to Storyblok's partnerships or ecosystem team and lead with the Otterly.AI connection. Storyblok and Prerender already share a mutual partner in the AI search space, which makes the outreach warmer than a cold approach. The pitch centers on a clear gap Storyblok clients face: their content is structured and beautifully managed, but if the frontend is JavaScript-heavy, AI crawlers and search engines may still not be able to read it. Prerender closes that gap without touching the CMS or the frontend. The goal in the first phase is a listed integration in Storyblok's technology ecosystem and a co-authored piece of content targeting their developer and marketer audience on AI search visibility.

6-Month Solution Partner Activation Plan
Sign → Certify → Announce → Activate → Keep The Momentum
This 6-month activation plan is designed to move partners from signed to revenue-producing by building the enablement, habits, and joint motion needed to turn partnerships into a repeatable, scalable acquisition channel, not just an opportunistic referral program. Partners need to understand the product deeply enough to demo it confidently, and understand the sales motion well enough to identify the right clients, frame the value proposition, communicate the unique selling points, and handle objections. Everything after that is about keeping momentum and making it easy for partners to expand the offering across a broader part of their client base.
Phase 1: Onboarding & Agreement  ·  Weeks 1–3
  • →Once the partnership agreement is signed, send a welcome email with access to the partner resource repository: product overview, ICP guide, battle card, sales enablement materials, training materials, and ROI calculator
  • →Partner completes a short expertise survey so Prerender can personalise enablement based on their tech stack, client profile, and service focus
  • →Kick-off call in the first week: walk through the program, demo the product, communicate the ICP and USP, and align on a shared goal for the first 90 days
  • →Prerender team creates the partner's directory listing, ready to go live at announcement
Phase 2: Certification  ·  Weeks 3–8
  • →Partner works through the certification program: courses covering product, ICP, sales motion, and objection handling, and passes the final assessment
  • →A check-in midway through certification to answer questions and keep momentum; ideally two team members certified, not just one (one sales certification and one technical)
  • →On completion, partner is added to Prerender's certified partner list and the partnership badge goes live on their site
Phase 3: Announcement  ·  Once Certified
  • →Joint blog post published simultaneously on both sites, co-branded social posts, and where applicable a shared email blast, all on the same day
  • →Partner directory listing goes live and the partnership is publicly visible on both sides
Phase 4: Early Revenue Support  ·  Months 2–3
  • →Light-touch follow-up after announcement to check on warm prospects and answer any deal-related questions, via email or a short call upon request
  • →Prerender joins sales calls on request during the first 90 days; this is a short-term support mechanism, not a permanent arrangement; the goal is for partners to run demos independently as they gain confidence
  • →Demo script, recorded walkthrough, and sandbox account provided so partners can run product demos without needing Prerender on the call
  • →Extended trial available at partner manager discretion for qualified, high-value prospects, not a default offer for every referral
Phase 5: Co-Marketing & Growth  ·  Months 2–6+
  • →Around month 2–3, begin planning the first co-marketing initiative
  • →Hold a call with the partner to showcase the co-marketing playbook: available formats (events, webinars, ebooks, co-branded service campaigns, guest blog posts, case studies, podcast), mutual benefits, MDF access (not yet in place), and the process for campaign creation
  • →Pick a format that suits the partner, agree on a topic aligned with Prerender's strategy, and set a launch date
  • →MDF available for approved activities; regular email check-in to review what's working and what to prioritise next
Phase 6: Partner Momentum & Reactivation  ·  Ongoing
  • →Segment partners monthly into three buckets: Active, At Risk, and Inactive, based on referrals, pipeline movement, and co-marketing participation
  • →For Inactive partners: run a reactivation outreach sequence: share one relevant narrative, propose one specific co-marketing option from the playbook, and ask for a single next action with a deadline
  • →For At Risk partners: run a quick reset call to identify blockers, re-align on ICP, and agree on one low-effort action to ship within the next 30 days
  • →For Active partners: reinforce and scale: give them priority directory placement, faster deal support, early access to new messaging, and first pick of co-marketing slots
  • →Top up partner materials continuously: slide decks, social media post templates, fresh use cases, campaign templates, and ready-to-send outreach copy partners can reuse with clients
  • →Launch a partner newsletter on a consistent cadence to keep partners warm and active: product updates, new assets, partner wins, upcoming initiatives, co-marketing opportunities, program results achieved together, and what is next in the pipeline
  • →Keep feedback loops tight: collect partner input on what is working, what is missing, and what would help them refer more, then update the playbook accordingly
Key Activation Metrics
Time-to-Deal
How fast a new partner generates their first referred deal.
Co-Mkt Leads
Leads generated through joint campaigns per year.
Activation Rate
Share of signed partners who refer a deal or run a co-marketing activity within 90 days.
Pipeline
Partner-sourced pipeline value: total $ of open deals that originated from a partner referral or co-marketing lead.
MRR %
Share of new monthly recurring revenue sourced by a partner. North star for the program's scorecard.

Prerender.io Announces Strategic Partnership with Storyblok
March 2026 · 2 min read

Prerender.io, one of the world's leading JavaScript rendering solutions, has announced a strategic partnership with Storyblok, a renowned headless CMS platform used by brands including Virgin Media O2, Oatly, and TomTom. Together, the two companies share a commitment to closing the gap between how content is created and how it is discovered by search engines and generative AI tools.

Headless CMS platforms like Storyblok have transformed the way teams build and deliver digital experiences. Yet as JavaScript-heavy frontends have become the standard, a meaningful discoverability gap has emerged, content that is beautifully structured and live for users remains inaccessible to search engines and AI crawlers like Googlebot, GPTBot, and ClaudeBot, which cannot reliably execute JavaScript.

Bridging the Gap Between Content Management and Search Visibility

Storyblok empowers marketers and developers to create, manage, and deliver structured content across any channel. Prerender.io ensures that content delivered through JavaScript-heavy frontends is rendered and fully visible to search engines and generative AI tools. Together, the two companies offer a complementary solution: Storyblok manages the content layer; Prerender manages the discoverability layer. When a search engine or AI crawler visits a Storyblok-powered site, Prerender intercepts the request and returns a complete HTML snapshot, ready for indexing, with no impact on the user experience and no changes required to the CMS or frontend.

“AI search is reshaping how audiences discover content, and our customers need confidence that what they build in Storyblok will be found. This partnership with Prerender.io addresses a genuine need, making sure nothing gets lost between content creation and search visibility.” — [Storyblok Executive]

Both companies are committed to deepening this collaboration as AI-powered search continues to reshape content discovery. For teams building on Storyblok, the combination means content is not only structured and ready to publish, but fully visible to every crawler that matters.

About Prerender.io — Prerender.io is a technical SEO solution that helps websites ensure their JavaScript content is fully visible to search engines and generative AI tools. Used by thousands of companies worldwide, including Microsoft and Walmart, Prerender.io solves critical SEO challenges for modern websites.
